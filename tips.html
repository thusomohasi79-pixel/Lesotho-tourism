Plan Your Perfect Trip 🇱🇸

Important tips to make your Lesotho experience smooth and enjoyable

🌤 Best Time to Visit

Spring and summer offer the best weather for exploring mountains and outdoor activities.

🚙 Transport

Use a 4x4 vehicle for mountain roads like Sani Pass for a safe and comfortable trip.

👘 Culture

Respect Basotho traditions and dress modestly when visiting villages.

💵 Currency

The local currency is the Loti, but South African Rand is also accepted.

✅ Do’s

  • Respect local customs
  • Carry warm clothes
  • Interact with locals

❌ Don’ts

  • Don’t litter
  • Don’t disrespect culture
  • Don’t travel unprepared

💡 Travel Smart

Always check weather conditions before traveling to mountainous areas. Safety and preparation are key to enjoying Lesotho.
